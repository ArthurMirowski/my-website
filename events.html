Weekly Activities

Join us for our activities every week at Light Disarray! There's always something to enjoy.

Run Club

Day: Every Wednesday

Time: 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Description: Lace up your sneakers and join our run club! Our weekly run around the nearby nature preserve is a great way to stay active and meet new people.

Group Bike Ride

Day: Every Saturday

Time: 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Description: Join us for a morning bike ride through the scenic routes around Light Disarray. It's a great way to kickstart your weekend!

Live DJ Set

Day: Every Friday and Saturday

Time: 10:00 PM - 2:00 AM

Description: Every weekend, we bring in local and touring DJs for a live performance. The beats, the atmosphere, the crowd, it's always an unforgettable night!
