About Light Disarray

Light Disarray is a unique café by day and a vibrant bar and lounge by night. Nestled near a beautiful nature preserve, just a short drive from the heart of the bustling city, Light Disarray offers the perfect blend of nature and urban energy. With easy access to the outdoors, you can enjoy a cozy, open-air atmosphere during the day, sipping your favorite brews, including a selection of imported beers, while surrounded by the calming beauty of nature.

Since 2020, our inviting space has transformed from a peaceful café into a lively bar and lounge as the sun sets. During the day, join us for exciting events like our run club, group bike rides, and live DJ sets, and as night falls, the energy ramps up with live music performances and expertly crafted cocktails. Our passion for mixology takes center stage, offering innovative drinks alongside timeless classics.

What We Believe In

At Light Disarray, we believe in the power of community, creativity, and connecting with nature. Our aim is to create a welcoming space where everyone can come together to enjoy great drinks, music, and unforgettable moments. Whether you're here for coffee, cocktails, or a night of dancing under the stars, we are committed to providing an experience that you will love.

Our Services

We offer a variety of beverages to suit every taste. We prepare locally roasted coffee, delicious pastries, refreshing imported beers, and expertly crafted cocktails. Our mixology team is dedicated to creating new and exciting drinks, from classics to innovative twists on your favorites. And don't forget to check out our live music events, where we feature talented artists and invite you to dance the night away!

Summary

Whether you're looking for a peaceful retreat to enjoy nature during the day or seeking a lively spot for a fun night out, Light Disarray has something special for everyone. Join us and become part of our vibrant community, where great drinks, music, and unforgettable moments come together in harmony with nature.

What's More?

Whether you're visiting us for a morning coffee, enjoying a leisurely lunch, or dancing the night away, our space reflects both tranquility and energy. We invite you to explore our Testimonials page to hear from our loyal guests, and if you're curious about our offerings, don't hesitate to reach out through our Contact Us form. Also, be sure to visit our Hours page to plan your next visit.
